Best outdoor fire pits for 2026

Updated June 2026Independently researchedNo paid placement.

Quick answer

For most people, the Solo Stove Bonfire 2.0 is the best outdoor fire pit for 2026, combining near-smoke-free burning with durable stainless steel construction.

The Outland Living Series 401 is a great runner-up if you prefer propane convenience, while the Heininger offers impressive heat output at a budget-friendly price.

A backyard fire pit turns ordinary evenings into memorable gatherings, roasting marshmallows, sharing stories, or just staring into the flames. But the right choice depends on your space, fuel preference, and patience for setup. Wood fires give you that classic crackle and scent but require cleaning and a safe spot; propane models light instantly and leave no ash, though you sacrifice some ambiance. When shopping, start with fuel type, then size: make sure the pit fits your patio or yard and meets any local burn bans. Build quality matters, look for thick steel or stainless steel, and check whether the base will rust. Finally, consider smoke output: double-wall designs and secondary combustion drastically reduce smoke, while open bowls produce plenty of atmosphere (and tears).
